About Me

I am an ambitious 3D artist, game developer, web developer, and interactive experience designer. I am currently pursuing a degree in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology at Boise State University.


Through this process, I have obtained a wide variety of skills in various areas, such as programming, game design, photography, video, audio, web design, and UX design. I am proficient in the Unity engine, being able to program 2D, 3D, AR and VR games and interactive experiences, although I am most passionate about 3D modeling and animation, as well as VFX. For this, I primarily am proficient in Maya, and occasionally use Blender, as well as Photoshop and Adobe Substance Painter 3D.


Outside of technology, I am also a multi-faceted musician. I play a wide variety of instruments, however I mainly play guitar, piano, and drums. I also have many outdoor hobbies as well, such as running, biking, snowboarding, and longboarding.
